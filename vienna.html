Urban Evolution of Vienna

Vienna, the capital of Austria, has a rich history dating back to Roman times. Over the centuries, it has evolved from a small settlement into a major European cultural and political center. The city's layout has been shaped by various historical events, including the Ottoman sieges, the Habsburg monarchy, and modern urban development. The maps presented here illustrate Vienna's transformation from the 15th to the 18th century, highlighting key changes in its fortifications, street patterns, and significant landmarks.
